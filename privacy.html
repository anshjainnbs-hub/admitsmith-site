Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

AdmitSmith ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website and services, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We only collect what we need to prepare your documents:

  • Contact details — your name, email, and phone number.
  • Application information — the academic, professional, and personal details you voluntarily share in our questionnaire so we can write your Statement of Purpose and related documents.
  • Order details — the package you purchased and payment confirmation (we do not store your card or bank details).

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to prepare and deliver your documents, process your order, and communicate with you about it. That's it.

We never sell or share your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. We only use the limited service providers required to operate:

  • Form delivery — questionnaire responses are delivered to us via FormSubmit.
  • Payments — processed by Razorpay, which handles your payment details under its own privacy policy.

These providers may only process your data to provide their service to us, never for their own purposes.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to deliver your order and provide support. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

Security

Our site is served over HTTPS and we treat your information as strictly confidential. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us at hello@admitsmith.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
